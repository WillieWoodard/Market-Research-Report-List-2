<p><p>An industrial gas cylinder is a container designed to store and transport gases at high pressure. The rising use of gases such as oxygen, nitrogen, argon, and carbon dioxide for various applications is driving the market growth. Additionally, the growing healthcare sector and the increasing use of medical gases are further fueling the demand for industrial gas cylinders.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the industrial gas cylinder market is the adoption of lightweight composite cylinders. These cylinders are lighter and more portable than traditional steel cylinders, making them suitable for applications where mobility is essential. Moreover, the use of advanced materials in composite cylinders enhances safety by providing better resistance to impact and corrosion.</p><p>Another trend in the market is the increasing use of advanced gas cylinder monitoring systems.
