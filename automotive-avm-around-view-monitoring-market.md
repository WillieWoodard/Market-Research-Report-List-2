<p><p>Automotive AVM (Around View Monitoring) is a technology used in vehicles to provide a 360-degree view of the surroundings, offering the driver a complete and real-time view of their environment. This system utilizes multiple cameras placed strategically around the vehicle to capture images, which are then stitched together to create an accurate and seamless view of the vehicle's surroundings. Automotive AVM enhances the overall driving experience, making parking and maneuvering much easier and safer.</p><p>The future outlook of the Automotive AVM market is highly promising. The increasing demand for advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS) and the growing focus on vehicle safety are major factors contributing to the market's growth. The rising adoption of autonomous and electric vehicles is also expected to drive the demand for AVM systems, as they play a crucial role in increasing the safety and efficiency of these vehicles.</p><p>Additionally, the integration of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Internet of Things (IoT) technologies in automotive AVM systems is further fueling market growth. These technologies enable advanced features such as object detection, blind-spot monitoring, and collision avoidance, providing even greater safety and convenience to drivers.</p><p>Moreover, the increasing awareness and implementation of stringent government regulations regarding vehicle safety and the growing consumer preference for advanced safety features are propelling the demand for automotive AVM systems.</p><p>Overall, the Automotive AVM market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. The market's expansion will be driven by the increasing adoption of ADAS, advancements in AI and IoT technologies, and the growing emphasis on vehicle safety.</p></p>

It is offered as a standard or optional feature by vehicle manufacturers. On the other hand, the Aftermarket market involves the installation of AVM systems after purchasing the vehicle. These systems are typically provided by third-party manufacturers and can be retrofitted onto vehicles that do not have the OEM AVM feature.</p></p>
