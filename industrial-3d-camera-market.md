<p><p>The Industrial 3D Camera Market comprises two main types: Area Scan Camera and Line Scan Camera. Area scan cameras capture 2D images of a specific area at once, allowing for bulk analysis and measurement of static objects. On the other hand, line scan cameras capture images line by line, enabling real-time inspection of moving objects. Both types find applications in industrial quality control, robotics, and automation, where precise and efficient imaging of objects is crucial for product evaluation and process optimization.</p></p>
