<p><p>The Vehicle ADAS Camera Lens Market includes CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) Cameras and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) Cameras. CCD cameras use a technology where light is converted into electric charge, resulting in high-quality images with low noise levels and excellent light sensitivity. On the other hand, CMOS cameras utilize a different technology, where light photons directly generate an electrical charge, offering advantages like lower power consumption, compact size, and faster data transfer. Both CCD and CMOS cameras are widely used in vehicle Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS) for improved safety and driver assistance capabilities.</p></p>
