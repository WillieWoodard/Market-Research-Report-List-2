<p><ul><li>Non-bioabsorbable</li><li>Bioabsorbable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The biotextiles market can be classified into two types: non-bioabsorbable and bioabsorbable. Non-bioabsorbable biotextiles are those that do not undergo any significant degradation or absorption within the body over time. They are designed to provide long-term support and strength to tissues or organs. On the other hand, bioabsorbable biotextiles are specifically engineered to degrade and get absorbed by the body over time. These biotextiles are used for temporary applications such as wound closure or tissue scaffolding, where long-term support is not required.</p></p>
