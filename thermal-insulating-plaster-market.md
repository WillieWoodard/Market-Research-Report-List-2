<p><p>The thermal insulating plaster market consists of two main types: expanded recycled glass beads based and cement binder based. Expanded recycled glass beads based plaster is made from recycled glass beads that are expanded through a heating process. This type of plaster is lightweight and offers excellent thermal insulation properties. On the other hand, cement binder based plaster is made by mixing cement with additives such as lightweight aggregates. It provides both thermal insulation and structural support, making it suitable for a wide range of applications.</p></p>
