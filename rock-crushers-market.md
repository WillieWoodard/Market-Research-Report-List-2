<p><p>Rock crushers are machines used to crush rocks into smaller sizes for various applications. Jaw crushers are most commonly used in mining or quarrying operations, crushing large rocks into smaller sizes. Cone crushers are similar but have a rotating mantle that crushes rocks by squeezing them between the mantle and a concave surface. Impact crushers use high-speed rotating hammers or blow bars to impact and shatter rocks. Each type of crusher offers distinct advantages depending on the specific crushing requirements and desired end product.</p></p>
