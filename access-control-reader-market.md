<p><p>The Access Control Reader market consists of three main types: card-based readers, biometric readers, and multi-technology readers. Card-based readers use identification cards, such as key cards or smart cards, to grant access. Biometric readers, on the other hand, rely on unique physical characteristics like fingerprints or facial recognition for identification. Multi-technology readers combine both card-based and biometric technologies to provide enhanced security.
