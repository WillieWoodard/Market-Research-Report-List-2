<p><p>Berlese funnels are commonly used in entomology to study and collect small organisms from soil or leaf litter samples. There are three main market types of Berlese funnels. The standard Berlese funnel is a vertically arranged funnel with a heat source placed above the sample, allowing organisms to move away from the heat and into a collection container. Pop-up Berlese funnels are similar but have a collapsible design, allowing for easy transportation. Tray-type Berlese funnels consist of trays stacked on top of each other, allowing for multiple samples to be collected simultaneously.</p></p>
