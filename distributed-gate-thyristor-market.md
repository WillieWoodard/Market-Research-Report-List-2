<p><p>The Distributed Gate Thyristor (also known as DGThyristor) market can be categorized into two main types: Capsule Type and Stud Type. Capsule Type DGThyristors are compact and sealed units that provide high voltage and current handling capabilities. They are commonly used in power transmission and distribution systems. On the other hand, Stud Type DGThyristors have a stud-like structure and are typically utilized in industrial applications where high power and resistance to thermal stress are required. Both types of DGThyristors play a crucial role in various electrical systems, ensuring efficient power control and reliable operation.</p></p>
