CFRP (carbon fiber reinforced polymer) is made by combining carbon fibers with a polymer resin matrix. CMC (ceramic matrix composites) use ceramic fibers instead of polymer resin, making them more heat resistant. MMC (metal matrix composites) are created by embedding carbon fibers in a metal matrix, resulting in enhanced durability. Each type offers unique benefits that cater to specific automobile applications.</p></p>
