<p><p>Fiber Reinforced Plastic (FRP) Pipe market consists of various types such as Glass Reinforced Plastics (GRP), Glass Reinforced Epoxy (GRE), and Glass Reinforced Vinyl Ester (GRV). GRP pipes are made of thermosetting resins, glass fibers, and fillers, offering superior strength and resistance to corrosion. GRE pipes, made by combining epoxy resin with glass fibers, possess excellent mechanical and chemical properties. GRV pipes feature a vinyl ester resin matrix with glass fibers, providing exceptional resistance against harsh chemicals and extreme temperatures. These FRP pipes find applications in industries like oil and gas, water treatment, and chemical processing.</p></p>
