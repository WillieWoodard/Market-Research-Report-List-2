<p><p>Scrap loaders are machines used in waste management and recycling operations to load scrap materials onto trucks or other vehicles. There are two main types of scrap loaders: stationary and mobile. Stationary scrap loaders are fixed in one place and are typically used in facilities where large amounts of scrap materials are generated. Mobile scrap loaders, on the other hand, are designed to be easily moved around different locations and are commonly used in construction sites or smaller recycling operations. Both types serve the purpose of efficiently loading and transporting scrap materials for further processing or disposal.</p></p>
