<p><p>An Industrial PoE (Power over Ethernet) Managed Switch refers to a network switch used in industrial environments that allows for the management and control of networked devices. With the increasing adoption of IoT (Internet of Things) devices in industries, the demand for efficient and reliable network connectivity solutions is on the rise.
