<p><strong>The Fireproofing Hydraulic Fluid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HFA</li><li>HFB</li><li>HFC</li><li>HFD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fireproofing Hydraulic Fluid Market consists of different types of fluids known as HFA, HFB, HFC, and HFD. HFA is a water-based hydraulic fluid that provides fire resistance by forming steam when exposed to fire. HFB is an oil-in-water emulsion that also forms a steam barrier when in contact with fire. HFC, or water glycol, has a higher ignition temperature and is fire-resistant due to the water content. HFD is a synthetic hydraulic fluid that does not ignite easily and provides excellent fireproofing properties.</p></p>
