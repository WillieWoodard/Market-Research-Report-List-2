<p><p>The Power System State Estimator market consists of various methods used for estimating power system states. The Weighted Least Square (WLS) method is a popular technique that minimizes the difference between observed and estimated values, assigning weights to different measurements based on their reliability. The Interior Point (IP) method solves a mathematical optimization problem to obtain a feasible solution. Other methods in the market include Kalman filter, particle swarm optimization, and unscented Kalman filter, which are used for accurate estimation of power system states based on different algorithms and models.</p></p>
