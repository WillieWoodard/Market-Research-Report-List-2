<p><p>Parallel reaction station refers to a device used in laboratories to simultaneously perform various chemical reactions. In terms of market types, there are two: single control system and multiple control systems. In a single control system, all reactions are controlled by one central unit and are performed under the same conditions. In contrast, a multiple control systems market offers reaction stations with individual control units for each reaction, allowing different reactions to be conducted with varying parameters simultaneously. This flexibility enables researchers to optimize conditions for specific reactions, enhancing efficiency in the laboratory.</p></p>
