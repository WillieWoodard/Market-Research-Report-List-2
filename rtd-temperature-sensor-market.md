<p><p>RTD temperature sensors are employed in various industries for accurate temperature measurement. The screw-in mounting type sensors are designed to be screwed directly into the process or equipment for convenient installation. Wall-mount mounting sensors are fixed on walls or surfaces, suitable for monitoring ambient temperatures. Insertion mounting sensors are inserted directly into pipes or tanks for measuring the temperature of the liquid or gas within. Threaded mounting sensors feature threads that enable them to be screwed into a threaded opening. Other types include duct-mount sensors, designed specifically for temperature monitoring in ductwork.</p></p>
