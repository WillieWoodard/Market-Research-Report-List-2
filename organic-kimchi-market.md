Kkakdugi is a fiery, crunchy fermented radish kimchi, while Pa-kimchi consists of garlic chives fermented with spicy ingredients. Oi Sobagi is a cucumber kimchi stuffed with spicy seasonings and fermented. These different types of kimchi cater to diverse taste preferences, ensuring a wide range of options for the organic kimchi market.</p></p>
