<p><p>The selfie stick market offers three main types: remote-triggered selfie sticks, wired selfie sticks, and Bluetooth selfie sticks. Remote-triggered selfie sticks include a remote control that connects to the stick via infrared or radio frequency, allowing users to snap photos without physically touching their phone. Wired selfie sticks directly connect to the phone using a cable, enabling users to trigger the shutter by pressing a button on the stick's handle. Bluetooth selfie sticks connect wirelessly to the phone through Bluetooth technology, offering remote control features via a dedicated app.</p></p>
