<p><p>The Internet Protocol Private Branch Exchange (IP PBX) market encompasses various types of communication devices and technologies. SIP Phones refer to devices that use the Session Initiation Protocol to initiate and manage voice and video calls over an IP network. VoIP Phones enable voice communication over the internet, converting analog signals to digital packets. IP PBX Servers are software-based systems that manage the internal communication network within an organization. VoIP Gateways allow the integration of traditional telephone systems with IP-based networks, enabling seamless communication between the two technologies.</p></p>
