<p><p>The tensioner pulley market consists of two main types: manual tensioner and automatic tensioner. A manual tensioner requires manual adjustment to maintain the proper tension in a system, such as a belt or chain. It is commonly used in applications where the tension requirements are moderate and do not change frequently. In contrast, an automatic tensioner utilizes a self-adjusting mechanism to automatically maintain correct tension levels. This type is often preferred in applications where the tension requirements are high or fluctuate frequently, ensuring efficient and reliable operation of the system.</p></p>
