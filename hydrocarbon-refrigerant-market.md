With increasing concerns about global warming and the depletion of ozone layer, there is a growing demand for environmentally friendly refrigerants. Hydrocarbon refrigerants are considered a sustainable and eco-friendly solution as they have zero ozone depletion potential and very low global warming potential.</p><p>Moreover, hydrocarbon refrigerants offer several advantages such as better energy efficiency, lower operating costs, and reduced carbon emissions. This is driving the adoption of hydrocarbon refrigerants in various end-use industries such as commercial refrigeration, industrial refrigeration, and air conditioning.</p><p>The current outlook of the hydrocarbon refrigerant market is also positive.
