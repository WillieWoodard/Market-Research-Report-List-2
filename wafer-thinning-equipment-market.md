<p><ul><li>Fully Automatic</li><li>Semi-automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wafer thinning equipment market can be classified into two types: fully automatic and semi-automatic. Fully automatic equipment refers to machines that operate without any manual intervention. They are designed to perform all tasks automatically, including loading and unloading wafers, adjusting the thickness, and monitoring the process. On the other hand, semi-automatic equipment involves a combination of automated and manual operations. While certain tasks are automated, there is still a requirement for human intervention, such as loading and unloading wafers. Both types of equipment cater to the thinning process of wafers but differ in the level of automation involved.</p></p>
