<p><ul><li>Serpentine Tube</li><li>Manifold Tube</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Power Battery Liquid Cold Plate market is categorized into three types: Serpentine Tube, Manifold Tube, and Others. </p><p>Serpentine Tube design involves a continuous and undulating flow path for the cooling fluid. This design allows for efficient heat transfer and uniform cooling of battery cells.</p><p>Manifold Tube design consists of multiple parallel tubes with a common inlet and outlet, enabling even distribution of the cooling fluid across the battery cells. This design ensures effective cooling performance.</p><p>The "Others" category includes alternative designs that may not fit into the above two categories, such as plate fin, straight tube, or microchannel design.</p></p>
