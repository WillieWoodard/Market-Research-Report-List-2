<p><p>Spark machining, also known as Electrical Discharge Machining (EDM), is a process used in metalworking that involves the removal of material through controlled electric discharges. There are three types of Spark Machining markets: Sinker EDM, Wire EDM, and Hole Drilling EDM. Sinker EDM uses electrodes to create a spark and remove material from the workpiece, while Wire EDM operates by feeding a thin wire through the workpiece to create the desired shape. Hole Drilling EDM, as the name suggests, is used specifically for drilling holes in the workpiece. These methods offer high precision and accuracy in the manufacturing industry.</p></p>
