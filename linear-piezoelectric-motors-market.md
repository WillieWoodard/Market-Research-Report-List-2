<p><p>The linear piezoelectric motors market comprises two main types: AC piezoelectric motors and DC piezoelectric motors. AC piezoelectric motors operate through the application of alternating current, generating vibrations that propel the motor. On the other hand, DC piezoelectric motors utilize direct current, resulting in linear motion through the expansion and contraction of piezoelectric elements. Both types offer precise positioning, high acceleration, and quick response, making them suitable for various industrial and technological applications.</p></p>
