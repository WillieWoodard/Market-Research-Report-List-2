<p><p>A servo drive system is a type of system used in industrial automation to control and power servo motors. The pulse market refers to the use of pulse signals to control the movement of the servo motor. In this type of system, the servo drive receives discrete on/off signals to determine motor movement. Analog servo drive systems operate based on analog signals, where the voltage level determines the motor position and speed. Network servo drive systems use a network communication protocol, such as Ethernet, to control multiple servo drives simultaneously through a centralized controller.</p></p>
