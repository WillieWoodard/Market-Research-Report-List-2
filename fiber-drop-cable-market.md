<p><ul><li>Single Mode Cable</li><li>Multi Mode Cable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber drop cable market is divided into two types: single mode cable and multi-mode cable. Single mode cables have a smaller core size, allowing only one mode of light to travel through the fiber. These cables are typically used for long-distance transmissions and are known for their higher bandwidth and longer reach. On the other hand, multi-mode cables have a larger core size, allowing multiple modes of light to travel through the fiber. They are mostly used for shorter distances and are known for their lower bandwidth and shorter reach.</p></p>
