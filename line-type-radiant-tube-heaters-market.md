<p><p>The line type radiant tube heater market consists of two main types: high-intensity radiant tube heaters and low-intensity radiant tube heaters. High-intensity radiant tube heaters produce a concentrated heat by emitting infrared radiation, suitable for areas with high heat requirements. Low-intensity radiant tube heaters, on the other hand, offer a more dispersed heat through a larger heated surface area, making them suitable for moderate-heat applications.
