<p><p>The automatic wafer bonding equipment market can be broadly classified into two types: fully automatic and semi-automatic. Fully automatic equipment refers to machines that perform all bonding processes without the need for human intervention. They possess advanced capabilities like automated material handling, alignment, and bonding. On the other hand, semi-automatic equipment requires some level of human involvement during the bonding process. Although they may have automated features, they still require operators to operate certain functions manually. Both types cater to the growing demand for efficient and precise wafer bonding processes in various industries.</p></p>
