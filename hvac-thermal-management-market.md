<p><p>The HVAC Thermal Management market includes two main types of systems: cooling systems and heating systems. Cooling systems are responsible for removing heat from a particular area or space, typically using air conditioning units or refrigeration systems. These systems help maintain comfortable temperatures and control humidity levels. On the other hand, heating systems are designed to increase the temperature in a given space, usually by using heating devices such as boilers, furnaces, or heat pumps. Both cooling and heating systems are essential for maintaining a comfortable and regulated indoor environment.</p></p>
