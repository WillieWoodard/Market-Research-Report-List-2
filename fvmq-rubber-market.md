The FVMQ rubber market can be classified into three types: liquid FVMQ, solid FVMQ, and semisolid FVMQ. </p><p>Liquid FVMQ refers to a form of FVMQ rubber that is in a liquid state, making it suitable for applications such as coatings, adhesives, and molding. Solid FVMQ, on the other hand, is a solid form used for producing various products like seals, gaskets, and o-rings. Semisolid FVMQ falls in between liquid and solid forms, often used for applications that require a more viscous consistency.
