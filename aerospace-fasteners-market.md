<p><p>The aerospace fasteners market consists of two main types: threaded fasteners and non-threaded fasteners. Threaded fasteners are commonly used in aerospace applications and include screws, bolts, and nuts that have threads to create a secure connection. They are designed to bear higher loads and provide strong joints in aircraft structures. On the other hand, non-threaded fasteners do not have threads and are used for specific applications such as rivets and pins, providing a more permanent and robust connection in aerospace assemblies. Both types are essential for maintaining the structural integrity and safety of aerospace components.</p></p>
