<p><p>Delivery drones are classified into two main types: rotary-wing and fixed-wing. Rotary-wing drones, also known as multicopters, are capable of vertical takeoff and landing, utilizing multiple rotors for lift and control. These drones offer great maneuverability and can hover in place, making them suitable for urban areas and short-distance deliveries. On the other hand, fixed-wing drones have a fixed wing structure and resemble traditional airplanes. They are designed for longer distances and use forward motion to generate lift, enabling them to cover larger areas quickly.</p></p>
