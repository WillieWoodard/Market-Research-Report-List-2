<p><strong>The Farm Tires Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bias Tire</li><li>Radial Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Farm tires are categorized into two types: Bias Tires and Radial Tires. Bias Tires are constructed with angled ply layers, offering great stability and strength. They are durable and suitable for heavy-duty farm equipment. On the other hand, Radial Tires are built with cord plies laid at a 90-degree angle to the direction of travel, providing better traction and fuel efficiency. They are more flexible and gentle on the soil, reducing soil compaction. Both types cater to different farming needs, ensuring efficient performance and productivity on the field.</p></p>
