<p><p>The Reflective Holographic Grating market includes two types: Flat Type and Concave Type. The Flat Type is a holographic grating that possesses a flat surface, providing uniform diffraction of light across its entire surface area. It is commonly used in applications where dispersion and high efficiency are required. On the other hand, the Concave Type is a holographic grating that possesses a concave surface. It focuses the light by diffracting it inward, resulting in enhanced resolution and increased efficiency, making it suitable for various spectroscopy and monochromator applications.</p></p>
