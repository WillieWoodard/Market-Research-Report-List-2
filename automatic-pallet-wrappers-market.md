<p><p>In the market of automatic pallet wrappers, there are two main types: semi-automatic and full-automatic. Semi-automatic pallet wrappers require some manual assistance from the operator, such as attaching the film to the pallet and initiating the wrapping process. On the other hand, full-automatic pallet wrappers operate independently, without the need for manual intervention. They usually have advanced features like automatic film cutting and attachment, making them more efficient and convenient for high-volume palletizing operations. Both types cater to different levels of automation and offer varying degrees of efficiency and ease of use.</p></p>
