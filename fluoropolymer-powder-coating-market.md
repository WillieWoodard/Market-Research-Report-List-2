<p><strong>The Fluoropolymer Powder Coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermoplastic Powder Coatings</li><li>Thermosetting Powder Coatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fluoropolymer powder coatings are divided into two types: thermoplastic powder coatings and thermosetting powder coatings. </p><p>Thermoplastic powder coatings are composed of polymers that can be melted and re-melted multiple times without losing their chemical structure. These coatings have excellent adhesion properties and are often used for coating heat-sensitive substrates.</p><p>On the other hand, thermosetting powder coatings undergo a chemical reaction when heated, permanently setting their structure. This makes them highly durable and resistant to heat, chemicals, and weathering. Thermosetting coatings are commonly used in applications requiring long-term protection and durability.</p></p>
