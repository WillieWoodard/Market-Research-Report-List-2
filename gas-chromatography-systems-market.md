<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Gas chromatography systems market types include gas-solid chromatography (GSC) and gas-liquid chromatography (GLC). GSC involves the separation of components of a mixture based on their interaction with a solid stationary phase, while GLC separates components based on their distribution between a liquid stationary phase and a gas mobile phase. Both methods utilize a chromatographic column, where the sample is introduced and separated. These techniques are widely used in various industries, such as pharmaceuticals, healthcare, and environmental analysis, for accurate and efficient separation, identification, and quantification of components in a mixture.</p></p>
