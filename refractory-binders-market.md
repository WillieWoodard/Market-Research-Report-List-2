<p><p>Refractory binders are materials used to hold together the particles in refractory products, such as bricks and castables, during high-temperature applications. High alumina binders are made from alumina-containing minerals and offer excellent resistance to heat, erosion, and chemical attacks. Colloidal silica binders, on the other hand, are formed by suspending small silica particles in water and provide good strength and adhesion properties. Both types of binders are crucial in the refractory industry as they contribute to the overall performance and durability of refractory products in various industrial processes.</p></p>
