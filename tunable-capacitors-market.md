<p><p>Tunable capacitors are electronic components that can adjust their capacitance value. Air gap tunable capacitors have a variable air gap between their plates, allowing for capacitance adjustment. Vacuum tunable capacitors use a variable vacuum between the plates, ensuring stable performance. SF6 gas filled tunable capacitors utilize SF6 gas to modify the capacitance. These different types of tunable capacitors offer flexibility in designing circuits and systems, enabling precise tuning and control of capacitance levels for various applications in electronics and telecommunications.</p></p>
