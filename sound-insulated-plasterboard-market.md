<p><p>Sound-insulated plasterboard is a specialized type of building material that is designed to provide enhanced sound insulation properties. It is made by sandwiching a layer of sound-absorbing material, such as gypsum or mineral wool, between two layers of plasterboard. This construction helps in reducing noise transmission between rooms, making it ideal for use in residential and commercial buildings, particularly in areas where noise control is crucial, such as bedrooms, offices, and recording studios.</p><p>The future outlook for the sound-insulated plasterboard market is expected to be positive. The increasing focus on noise pollution control and the growing demand for soundproofing solutions in commercial and residential buildings are driving the market growth.
