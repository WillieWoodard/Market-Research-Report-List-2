<p><p>The electric hedge trimmer market consists of two main types: single-sided and double-sided trimmers. Single-sided trimmers have blades on one side of the tool, making them ideal for trimming hedges along a straight edge or a wall. They are known for their precision and are typically lighter and more affordable. On the other hand, double-sided trimmers have blades on both sides, providing increased efficiency and versatility. They are suitable for shaping and trimming hedges from different angles, offering faster results but often at a higher price.</p></p>
