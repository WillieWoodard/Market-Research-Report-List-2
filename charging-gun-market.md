<p><p>The charging gun market can be categorized into two types: DC charging guns and AC charging guns. DC charging guns are primarily used for fast charging electric vehicles (EVs) as they provide direct current to the vehicle's battery. They are commonly found in public charging stations or at EV service centers. On the other hand, AC charging guns are suited for slower charging requirements, like overnight charging at home or workplace. They convert alternating current from the power supply into direct current for EVs. Both types cater to the diverse charging needs of EV owners.</p></p>
