<p><p>The linerless paper market consists of three types: direct thermal, thermal transfer, and laser. Direct thermal technology involves heat-sensitive paper that produces an image when heat is applied. It is commonly used for labels, tickets, and receipts. Thermal transfer uses a ribbon to transfer ink onto the paper, offering higher durability and resistance to environmental conditions. Laser technology utilizes heat from a laser beam to create an image on the paper, often used for high-quality printing requirements. These different types cater to diverse applications and provide varying benefits for users.</p></p>
