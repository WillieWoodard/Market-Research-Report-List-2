<p><p>The automotive panoramic roof market consists of two main types: multi-panel sunroofs and single panel sunroofs. Multi-panel sunroofs are designed with multiple glass panels, allowing for separate opening and closing of each section. This offers passengers the flexibility to control the amount of light and ventilation they desire. On the other hand, single panel sunroofs are made up of one large glass panel that can be opened or closed in its entirety, providing a seamless and unobstructed view of the sky and adding a sense of spaciousness to the vehicle's interior.</p></p>
