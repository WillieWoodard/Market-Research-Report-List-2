<p><p>Screw Pumps are categorized into three main types based on the number of screws they contain. Single Screw Pump, as the name suggests, consists of one screw encased within a cylindrical cavity. Twin-Screw Pump contains two co-rotating screws inside a double-helical cavity. Multiple Screw Pump incorporates more than two screws within its structure, typically three or more. Each type of screw pump is used to efficiently move liquids or viscous materials, ensuring smooth and reliable operation in various industries.</p></p>
