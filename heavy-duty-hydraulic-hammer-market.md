These hammers are typically used in construction, mining, and demolition applications. Towers, on the other hand, are hydraulic hammers that are designed with a tower-like structure, enabling them to deliver high impact force for breaking large rocks and concrete structures. They are commonly used in road construction and quarrying industries.</p></p>
