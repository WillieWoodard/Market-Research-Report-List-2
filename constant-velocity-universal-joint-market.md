<p><strong>The Constant Velocity Universal Joint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ball</li><li>Fork</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Constant Velocity Universal Joint (CVUJ) market includes two types: the Ball market and the Fork market. The Ball market consists of CVUJs that use ball bearings to transmit torque smoothly between connected shafts. This design allows for high misalignment accommodation and reduces friction. On the other hand, the Fork market consists of CVUJs that utilize a forked yoke to transmit torque. This design offers good resistance to torsional vibrations and axial movements. Both types of CVUJs play vital roles in various industries where constant velocity transmission is crucial.</p></p>
