<p><p>Commercial Vehicle Telematics Market includes two main types: Hardware Equipment and Software System. Hardware Equipment refers to the physical devices installed in commercial vehicles, such as GPS trackers, sensors, and cameras, which collect and transmit data on vehicle location, speed, fuel consumption, and other parameters. Software System, on the other hand, encompasses the software applications that process and analyze the data collected by the hardware equipment.
