<p><p>The container wagon market includes two main types of wagons: axle articulated intermodal wagons and axle intermodal wagons. Axle articulated intermodal wagons consist of separate sections connected by articulated joints, allowing for smooth movement around curves. These wagons are more flexible and can handle different container sizes. On the other hand, axle intermodal wagons have a fixed structure with axles placed at regular intervals. They are more commonly used and offer a stable platform for container transportation. Both types play a significant role in the efficient movement of containers in the market.</p></p>
