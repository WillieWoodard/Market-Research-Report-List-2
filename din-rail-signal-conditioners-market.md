<p><p>DIN Rail Signal Conditioners are devices used in industrial applications to convert and amplify electronic signals. Single-Channel Signal Conditioners are designed to process one input signal at a time, providing conversion, isolation, and amplification. They are commonly used when dealing with individual sensors or instruments. On the other hand, Multi-Channel Signal Conditioners can handle multiple input signals simultaneously, making them suitable for applications requiring simultaneous processing of various signals from different sensors or instruments. These signal conditioners offer higher flexibility and efficiency in industrial automation systems.</p></p>
