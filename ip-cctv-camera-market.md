<p><p>The IP CCTV Camera market consists of two main types: Centralized IP Cameras and Decentralized IP Cameras. Centralized IP Cameras require a central network video recorder (NVR) to process and store footage. They are ideal for large-scale surveillance systems and offer advanced features like remote access and analytics. On the other hand, Decentralized IP Cameras have built-in recording capabilities, eliminating the need for an NVR. They are suitable for smaller setups and provide more flexibility and cost-effectiveness. Both types provide high-quality video surveillance with varying degrees of complexity and functionality.</p></p>
