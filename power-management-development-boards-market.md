<p><p>Power management development boards are used for testing and prototyping power electronic systems. IGBT development boards are specifically designed for applications that require high voltage and current ratings. They are commonly used in motor drives, inverters, and power supplies. Other markets for power management development boards include wide-bandgap semiconductor devices like silicon carbide (SiC) and gallium nitride (GaN), as well as emerging technologies like solid-state power converters and wireless power transfer.</p></p>
