<p><p>The plastic molding additives market includes various types of additives that are used during the plastic manufacturing process. Flame retardants reduce the flammability of the plastic, making it safer. Blowing agents are added to create a foam structure in the plastic. Nucleating agents enhance the crystallization process, improving the mechanical properties of the plastic. Other additives include colorants, UV stabilizers, and antistatic agents.</p></p>
