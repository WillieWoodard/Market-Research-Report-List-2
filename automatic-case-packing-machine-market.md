<p><strong>The Automatic Case Packing Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>V-FFS</li><li>H-FFS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic case packing machine market consists of two major types: V-FFS and H-FFS. The V-FFS (Vertical Form-Fill-Seal) machines are vertically designed and fill products into pre-made cases or cartons from the top. These machines are beneficial for packaging fragile or delicate products. On the other hand, H-FFS (Horizontal Form-Fill-Seal) machines are horizontally designed and fill products into cases or cartons from the side. These machines are suitable for packaging sturdy or heavy products. Both types offer automated packaging solutions but cater to different product requirements.</p></p>
