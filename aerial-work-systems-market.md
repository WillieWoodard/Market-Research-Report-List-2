Truck-mounted lifts are mounted on vehicles, providing mobility and enhanced reach. Lastly, the "others" category includes less common aerial work systems such as mast boom lifts, vertical personnel lifts, and trailer-mounted lifts. These systems cater to diverse needs in industries like construction, maintenance, and utilities.</p></p>
