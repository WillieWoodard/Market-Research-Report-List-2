<p><ul><li>Thermoset Polyimide</li><li>Thermoplastic Polyimide</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyimide (PI) plastics are a type of high-performance plastic used in various industries. They can be categorized into two market types: thermoset polyimide and thermoplastic polyimide. </p><p>Thermoset polyimide is a type of PI plastic that undergoes a curing process, forming a crosslinked molecular structure that cannot be melted or reshaped once solidified. It offers excellent thermal and chemical resistance, making it suitable for applications that require high temperature stability, such as in aerospace and electronics.</p><p>On the other hand, thermoplastic polyimide can be melted and reshaped multiple times without losing its properties. It also has good mechanical strength and chemical resistance. This type of PI plastic finds applications in industries like automotive, electrical, and medical where the ability to mold and form different shapes is required.</p></p>
