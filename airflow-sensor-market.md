<p><p>The airflow sensor market comprises various types of sensors used to measure the flow rate of air in different applications. Vane airflow sensors detect airflow based on the rotation of a small vane. Karman vortex airflow sensors measure the frequency of vortices shed by a bluff body in the airflow. Hot wire airflow sensors utilize a heated wire to measure the cooling effect of the passing air. Hot film airflow sensors rely on a heated film to detect changes in temperature caused by airflow. These different sensors cater to the varying needs of industries and applications.</p></p>
