<p><p>The automotive motor core market comprises different types, including welding motor core, riveting motor core, and brushless motor core. Welding motor cores are created by joining individual laminations through a welding process, ensuring a strong bond. Riveting motor cores, on the other hand, are formed by riveting or clipping the laminations together. Brushless motor cores do not require physical contact, instead, they use magnets to generate rotatory motion, making them more efficient and durable. These different market types cater to various automotive applications, offering options for manufacturers to meet specific requirements.</p></p>
