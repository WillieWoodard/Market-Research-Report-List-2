<p><p>Ruthenium metal is a rare transition metal that belongs to the platinum group of elements. The market is mainly driven by increasing demand from the electronics industry, where ruthenium is used in the manufacturing of electrical contacts, microelectronics, and thick-film resistors. The growth in the electronics industry, particularly in emerging economies, is expected to fuel the demand for ruthenium metal.</p><p>Furthermore, the automotive industry is also a key consumer of ruthenium metal. It is used in catalytic converters and emissions control devices to reduce harmful exhaust emissions. As automotive regulations become stricter globally, the demand for ruthenium in this sector is projected to increase.</p><p>Additionally, the growing demand for renewable energy sources such as fuel cells and solar cells has created new opportunities for ruthenium metal.
