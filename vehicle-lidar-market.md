<p><strong>The Vehicle LiDAR Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Lidar</li><li>Solid State Lidar</li><li>Hybrid Solid-state Lidar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vehicle LiDAR market is categorized into three types: Mechanical LiDAR, Solid State LiDAR, and Hybrid Solid-state LiDAR. Mechanical LiDAR systems use moving parts to steer the laser beams and provide accurate 3D mapping of the surrounding environment. Solid State LiDAR utilizes solid-state components like micro-electromechanical systems to generate and steer laser beams, offering a more compact and reliable solution. Hybrid Solid-state LiDAR combines the strengths of both mechanical and solid-state systems, offering improved performance and reliability in a compact design. All these types contribute to the advancement of autonomous vehicles and safety features.</p></p>
