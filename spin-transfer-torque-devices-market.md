<p><p>Spin-transfer torque devices are a type of magnetic memory technology that operates based on the transfer of angular momentum from an electron's spin to the magnetization of a magnetic layer. In the Clockwise Spin market, the electron's spin is aligned in the same direction as the magnetic layer's magnetization, resulting in a positive torque. Conversely, in the Counter Clockwise Spin market, the electron's spin is aligned in the opposite direction, generating a negative torque. These market types represent different strategies for manipulating and controlling magnetic memory in spin-transfer torque devices.</p></p>
