<p><p>Amorphous alloys market refers to the industry that deals with the production and utilization of materials without a defined crystalline structure. In terms of market types, there are two main categories: ordered structure and disordered structure. An ordered structure refers to alloys that have a well-defined arrangement of atoms, resulting in specific properties like high thermal stability. On the other hand, a disordered structure is characterized by random atomic arrangements, leading to unique features such as high strength and good magnetic properties. Both types cater to different industries and applications within the amorphous alloys market.</p></p>
