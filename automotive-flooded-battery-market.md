<p><ul><li>Serviceabl Battery</li><li>Maintenance Free Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive flooded battery market comprises two major types - serviceable battery and maintenance-free battery. Serviceable batteries require regular maintenance, such as topping up with distilled water to replenish electrolyte levels. These batteries typically have removable caps for easy access to cells. On the other hand, maintenance-free batteries do not require any regular maintenance, as they come equipped with a resealable vent cap, effectively eliminating the need for electrolyte replenishment. They are sealed, reducing the risk of acid leakage and providing a hassle-free experience for vehicle owners.</p></p>
