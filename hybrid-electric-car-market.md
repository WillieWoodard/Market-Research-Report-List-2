<p><p>The hybrid electric car market can be classified into three types: Series Hybrid Electric Cars, Parallel Hybrid Electric Cars, and Series-Parallel Hybrid Electric Cars. In Series Hybrid Electric Cars, the internal combustion engine charges the battery, which then powers an electric motor. Parallel Hybrid Electric Cars use both the engine and electric motor simultaneously or separately to drive the wheels. Series-Parallel Hybrid Electric Cars combine features of both previous types, allowing the engine to directly power the wheels or charge the battery, with the electric motor assisting when needed.</p></p>
