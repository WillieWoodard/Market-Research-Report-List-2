<p><p>DC Tachogenerators are devices that are used to measure the rotational speed of a motor or other machinery. There are two main types of DC Tachogenerators: Permanent Magnet Type and Electromagnetic Type. </p><p>Permanent Magnet Type Tachogenerators use a permanent magnet to generate a magnetic field and measure the speed based on the voltage output induced by the rotation of the magnet. </p><p>On the other hand, Electromagnetic Type Tachogenerators use electromagnetic fields created by electrical currents to measure the speed. They rely on the principle of electromagnetic induction to generate an output voltage proportional to the rotational speed.</p></p>
