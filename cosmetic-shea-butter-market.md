<p><p>Cosmetic Shea Butter is divided into three types based on its refining process. Grade A (Unrefined) Shea Butter is raw and pure, extracted manually and retains its natural nutty aroma and golden color. Grade B (Refined) Shea Butter undergoes a filtering process to remove impurities and deodorize it, resulting in a neutral scent and white color. Grade C (Highly Refined) Shea Butter undergoes further processing, including deodorization, chemical extraction, and bleaching, making it odorless and pure white. Each grade caters to different preferences and purposes in the cosmetic industry.</p></p>
