<p><p>Thyristor Surge Protection Devices (TSPD) are devices used to protect electronic equipment from power surges and transients. The market for TSPDs includes bidirectional and unidirectional types. Bidirectional TSPDs can handle surges in both directions of the current flow and are commonly used in applications where power can flow in either direction. On the other hand, unidirectional TSPDs are designed to handle surges in only one direction of the current flow and are typically used in applications where protection is needed for specific current flow directions.</p></p>
