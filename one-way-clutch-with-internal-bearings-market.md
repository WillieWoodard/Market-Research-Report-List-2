<p><p>One-way clutch with internal bearings refers to a mechanism that allows rotational movement in only one direction. It consists of a clutch and internal bearings. There are two main types of internal bearings used in this market: ball bearings clutches and roller bearings clutches. Ball bearings clutches use balls between the inner and outer races to transmit torque, while roller bearings clutches use cylindrical rollers for the same purpose. Both types serve the same function of enabling unidirectional rotation and find application in various industries.</p></p>
