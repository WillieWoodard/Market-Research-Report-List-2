<p><p>The kitchen paper towel market comprises two main types: wood pulp and primary slurry. Wood pulp refers to paper towels made from natural wood fibers, which are known for their absorbency and strength. On the other hand, primary slurry paper towels are made using a mixture of wood pulp and recycled paper materials. Primary slurry towels are often more cost-effective, but may have slightly lower quality compared to wood pulp towels. Both types of paper towels are commonly used in households and restaurants for various cleaning and wiping tasks.</p></p>
