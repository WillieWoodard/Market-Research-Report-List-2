<p><ul><li>Electro Chemical</li><li>Thermal Storage</li><li>Mechanical Energy Storage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Energy Storage System Market Types can be classified into three main categories:</p><p>1. Electrochemical Energy Storage: It involves the conversion of electrical energy into chemical energy through the use of batteries, fuel cells, or supercapacitors. These systems store and release energy by conducting reversible electrochemical reactions.</p><p>2. Thermal Storage: This type of storage involves the conversion of energy into heat and its subsequent release as needed. Common examples include hot water storage, phase change materials, and molten salt storage.</p><p>3. Mechanical Energy Storage: It includes storing energy in various mechanical forms such as compressed air, flywheels, or pumped hydro storage. These systems store energy by converting electrical energy into kinetic or potential energy, which can be released when needed.</p></p>
