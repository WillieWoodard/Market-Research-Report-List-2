<p><p>Appearance boards are widely used in construction and furniture-making industries. Plywood is a versatile and cost-effective option, made by layering thin sheets of wood veneer together. Medium density fiberboard (MDF) is engineered from wood fibers and offers a smooth surface and excellent dimensional stability. Particle board is composed of wood particles bonded together with resin, providing a cost-effective and consistent substrate. Block board has a core made of solid wood blocks and is suitable for applications requiring strength. Other types of appearance boards include oriented strand board (OSB), hardboard, and composite panels, each offering unique features and applications.</p></p>
