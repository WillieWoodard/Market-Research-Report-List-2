<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automatic Transmission Oil Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Gear Oil Pump</li><li>Rotary Oil Pump</li><li>Blade Oil Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic transmission oil pump market comprises three main types: internal gear oil pump, rotary oil pump, and blade oil pump. </p><p>The internal gear oil pump utilizes two gears to transfer the fluid, with one gear driving the other. It is commonly used in various applications due to its compact design and efficiency. </p><p>The rotary oil pump operates with a rotating rotor and vanes, creating pressure to propel the oil. It offers high volumetric efficiency and is suitable for heavy-duty applications. </p><p>The blade oil pump uses blades to create a vacuum effect, drawing in and propelling the oil. It is known for its low noise and vibration levels, making it suitable for automotive applications.</p></p>
