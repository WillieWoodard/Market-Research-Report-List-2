<p><p>The Bubble Level Gauge market types can be classified into non-contact type and contact type. Non-contact type bubble level gauges are designed to measure the inclination or tilt of an object without physically touching it. These gauges use advanced technologies like laser or infrared to determine the object's level. On the other hand, contact type bubble level gauges involve direct physical contact with the object being measured. These gauges typically consist of a glass tube filled partially with liquid and a bubble that moves along the tube to indicate the level.</p></p>
