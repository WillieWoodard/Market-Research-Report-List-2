<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Rod extensometers are devices used to measure the strain or deformation of structures. Single point rod extensometers consist of a rod with sensors at one point to measure deformation. They are suitable for monitoring small-scale deformations in single locations. On the other hand, multipoint rod extensometers have sensors at multiple points along the rod to measure strain variations at different locations simultaneously. They are used to monitor large-scale deformations in structures like dams, tunnels, and slopes. Both types play a vital role in ensuring the stability and safety of infrastructure projects.</p></p>
