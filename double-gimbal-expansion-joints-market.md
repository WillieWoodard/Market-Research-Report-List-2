<p><p>The double gimbal expansion joints market includes three main types: flange type, welding type, and groove type. Flange type joints are connected to flanged pipe ends, ensuring a secure and leak-proof connection. Welding type joints are welded directly to the pipes, providing a strong and durable connection. Groove type joints have a grooved design that allows for easy installation and disassembly, making them suitable for applications that require frequent joint adjustments or replacements. These different types cater to various needs and preferences within the market.</p></p>
