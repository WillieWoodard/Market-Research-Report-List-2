<p><p>The MF (Microfiltration) and UF (Ultrafiltration) membrane market can be classified into two types: organic and inorganic membranes. Organic MF&UF membranes are made from polymer materials like cellulose acetate and polyethersulfone. These membranes have pore sizes ranging from 0.1 to 0.01 micrometers and are commonly used for filtration applications in industries such as food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and biotechnology. On the other hand, inorganic MF&UF membranes are made from materials like ceramics and metals. They have smaller pore sizes (0.1 to 0.001 micrometers) and are preferred in wastewater treatment, desalination, and other industrial processes requiring finer filtration.</p></p>
