<p><p>The recycled polyester filament yarn market comprises two main types: solid fiber and hollow fiber. Solid fiber refers to polyester filaments that have a consistent, solid structure throughout their length. These filaments are widely used in apparel, home textiles, and industrial applications due to their strength and durability. Hollow fiber, on the other hand, is composed of polyester filaments with a hollow, tubular structure. This type of filament offers additional benefits such as lightweight properties, enhanced insulation, and improved breathability, making it suitable for applications in bedding, outdoor gear, and filtration products.</p></p>
