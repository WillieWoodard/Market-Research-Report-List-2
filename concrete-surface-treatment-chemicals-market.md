<p><strong>The Concrete Surface Treatment Chemicals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Curing Compounds</li><li>Mold Release Agents</li><li>Sealants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The concrete surface treatment chemicals market is segmented into three main types: curing compounds, mold release agents, and sealants. </p><p>Curing compounds are chemical coatings applied to new concrete surfaces to retain moisture, promoting better curing and preventing cracking. </p><p>Mold release agents are substances used to prevent the concrete from sticking to the molds or formwork during the casting process, facilitating easy and clean mold removal. </p><p>Sealants are applied to concrete surfaces to protect them from moisture, chemicals, and environmental damage, ensuring improved durability and longevity.</p></p>
