<p><p>Mold steel is categorized into three major types: cold work die steel, hot work die steel, and plastic mould steel. Cold work die steel is used for fabricating tools that shape or cut materials through cold processing. Hot work die steel is utilized for manufacturing tools that function under high temperatures and intense pressure. Plastic mould steel is employed for creating molds used in plastic injection molding processes. Each type of mold steel serves a specific purpose and offers unique properties to ensure efficient and effective mold fabrication.</p></p>
