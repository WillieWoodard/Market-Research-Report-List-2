<p><p>The automotive wrapping film market offers different types of films such as PET, PVC, and Vinyl Resin for various applications. PET films are made from thermoplastic polyester and are known for their high strength and clarity. PVC films are made from polyvinyl chloride and are popular for their durability and flexibility. Vinyl Resin films are made from a combination of vinyl chloride and vinyl acetate and offer excellent adhesion and weather resistance. These films are extensively used for wrapping vehicle exteriors to enhance their appearance and protect the original paint.</p></p>
