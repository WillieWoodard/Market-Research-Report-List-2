<p><p>The Magnetic Shield Power Inductors Market consists of two main types - Multilayer Power Inductors and Wirewound Power Inductors. Multilayer Power Inductors are compact in size and made of several layers of thin metallic materials, providing high inductance values. They are suitable for applications requiring small form factors. On the other hand, Wirewound Power Inductors are larger in size and constructed by winding a wire around a core material, offering enhanced power handling capabilities. They are commonly used in high-power applications that demand efficient energy storage and transfer.</p></p>
