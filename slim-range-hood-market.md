<p><p>The slim range hood market offers different types of hoods, each with its own unique features. Top suction range hoods are mounted above the cooking area and extract smoke and odors vertically. Downdraft hoods, on the other hand, are installed in the countertop and extract smoke and odors downwards through vents. These different types cater to varying kitchen layouts and allow for efficient removal of fumes while cooking.</p></p>
