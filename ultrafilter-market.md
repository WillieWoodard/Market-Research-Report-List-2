<p><p>Ultrafilters are used in various industries for filtration purposes. Tube-type ultrafilters are cylindrical in shape and typically consist of a membrane with a predetermined pore size to separate impurities. These are commonly used in water treatment systems to remove bacteria and microorganisms. On the other hand, roll-type ultrafilters consist of a flat sheet membrane material wound onto a core. They are typically employed in industries where a larger surface area is required for filtration, such as the pharmaceutical and biotech sectors. Both market types offer effective filtration solutions, but differ in their shape and application.</p></p>
