<p><ul><li>Reaction Torque Sensor</li><li>Rotary Torque Sensor</li><li>Super Acoustic Wave (SAW) Torque Sensor</li><li>Optical Torque Sensor</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Torque sensors are categorized into different types based on their functionality and application. Reaction torque sensors measure the torque acting on a stationary component in a system, while rotary torque sensors measure the torque transferred through a rotating shaft. Super Acoustic Wave (SAW) torque sensors utilize acoustic waves to measure torque accurately. Optical torque sensors employ optical principles to detect and quantify torque. Lastly, the "Others" category includes torque sensors that do not fit into the mentioned types but still offer unique capabilities for torque measurement in various industries and applications.</p></p>
