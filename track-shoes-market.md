<p><p>Track shoes market comprises of two main types: ordinary track shoes and spike shoes. Ordinary track shoes are designed with a flat rubber sole suitable for various track surfaces, providing adequate traction and support for athletes. Spike shoes, on the other hand, feature sharp spikes at the sole to increase grip and traction on the track, enhancing speed and performance. While ordinary track shoes offer versatility for different terrains, spike shoes are mostly preferred by professional athletes to optimize their speed and precision during races.</p></p>
