<p><strong>The Sliding Table Saw Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Driven Directly</li><li>Driven By Belt</li><li>Driven By Gears</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sliding table saws are classified into three market types based on how their sliding mechanism is driven. The first type is driven directly, where the sliding movement is powered by an electric motor connected directly to the sliding mechanism. The second type is driven by a belt, with the motor connected to the sliding mechanism through a belt drive system. The last type is driven by gears, where the motor's power is transmitted to the sliding mechanism through gears. These different types offer various advantages and can cater to different woodworking needs.</p></p>
