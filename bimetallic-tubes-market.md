<p><p>Bimetallic tubes are composite tubes made from two different metals or alloys that possess distinct physical and chemical properties. These tubes are primarily used in various industries, such as oil and gas, chemical, petrochemical, power generation, and automotive, due to their high resistance to corrosion, heat, and pressure. Bimetallic tubes are commonly employed in heat exchangers, condensers, boilers, and other applications that require superior performance and long-lasting durability.</p><p>Market analysis suggests that the global bimetallic tubes market is projected to exhibit substantial growth during the forecast period. The growing demand for high-quality heat exchangers and condensers in the oil and gas industry is a major driving factor for the market.
