<p><p>Juicer machines can be classified into three main types: centrifugal, masticating, and other markets. Centrifugal juicers utilize high-speed spinning blades to separate the juice from the pulp. They are known for their quick juicing process and are suitable for larger fruits and vegetables. On the other hand, masticating juicers use a slower and more thorough grinding process, which helps retain more nutrients and enzymes in the juice. Lastly, the other market includes various types like citrus juicers, manual juicers, and twin-gear juicers, which cater to specific juicing needs such as citrus fruits, manual operation, or more advanced functionalities.</p></p>
