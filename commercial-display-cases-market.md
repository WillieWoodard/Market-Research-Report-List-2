Chilling type display cases maintain a cool temperature to preserve perishable items like dairy products or fresh produce. Frozen type display cases are designed to keep items frozen, making them suitable for storing frozen foods like ice cream or frozen meat. Each type serves specific purposes within the commercial display cases market.</p></p>
