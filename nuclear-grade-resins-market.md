<p><p>The nuclear grade resins market can be categorized into three types: mixture nuclear grade resins, nuclear grade anion resins, and nuclear grade cationic resins. Mixture nuclear grade resins are a combination of anionic and cationic resins used in purification processes. Nuclear grade anion resins specifically remove negatively charged ions from nuclear power plant water systems. On the other hand, nuclear grade cationic resins eliminate positively charged ions. Each type of resin serves a specific purpose in the nuclear industry's water treatment processes, ensuring optimal performance and safety.</p></p>
