<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The molecular modelling market comprises two main types: the molecular mechanics approach and the quantum chemistry approach. In the molecular mechanics approach, molecules are represented by simplified force fields to simulate their behavior. It is mainly used for studying large systems and long-time behaviors. On the other hand, the quantum chemistry approach utilizes advanced mathematical models to calculate the electronic structure of molecules. This approach is more accurate but computationally intensive, making it suitable for studying smaller systems and short-time behaviors. Both approaches have their own strengths and are widely used in various research and development applications.</p></p>
