<p><p>The gas separation membrane market consists of different types of membranes for separating gases. Hollow fiber membranes are made up of a bundle of cylindrical fibers with high surface area, allowing efficient gas separation. Spiral wound membranes have a layered structure, with a membrane sheet wound around a permeate tube, leading to high efficiency and compact design. Other types of membranes include polymeric flat sheet, ceramic, and polyimide membranes. These membranes are used in various industries such as oil and gas, water treatment, pharmaceuticals, and food processing for gas separation applications.</p></p>
