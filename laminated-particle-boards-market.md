<p><p>Laminated Particle Boards are widely used materials in the construction industry. There are three main types: Raw Particleboard, Fire Resistant Particleboard, and Moisture Resistant Particleboard. Raw Particleboard is the most basic type and is commonly used as a substrate for laminating or veneering. Fire Resistant Particleboard has additives that enhance its resistance to fire, making it suitable for applications where fire safety is a concern. Moisture Resistant Particleboard is treated to withstand high levels of humidity and moisture, making it ideal for areas such as bathrooms and kitchens.</p></p>
