<p><p>The large circular knitting machine market consists of two main types: single circular knitting machines and double circular knitting machines. Single circular knitting machines are designed with a single needle bed and produce tubular knitted fabrics. They are commonly used for basic knitting patterns. On the other hand, double circular knitting machines have two synchronized needle beds and can produce fabric tubes with more complex designs and patterns. These machines are widely used in the textile industry for producing a variety of knitted garments and fabrics.</p></p>
