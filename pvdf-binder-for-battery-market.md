<p><p>PVDF (polyvinylidene fluoride) binder is used in battery manufacturing as a key component that adheres the electrode materials together. Emulsion polymerization and suspension polymerization are two common methods used to produce PVDF binders. Emulsion polymerization involves dispersing PVDF in water, followed by the addition of a surfactant to stabilize the particles. Suspension polymerization, on the other hand, involves dispersing PVDF in a liquid medium through agitation, followed by polymerization in the presence of a suspension agent. Both methods result in the production of PVDF binders for battery applications.</p></p>
