<p><p>The corrugated board market consists of four types: single face board, single wall board, double wall board, and triple wall board. Single face board has one flat side and one corrugated side. Single wall board has a flat liner on one side, a corrugated medium, and another flat liner on the other side. Double wall board has two layers of corrugated medium separated by flat liners. Triple wall board has three layers of corrugated medium separated by flat liners. These different types of boards cater to varying packaging needs in industries like retail, e-commerce, and logistics.</p></p>
