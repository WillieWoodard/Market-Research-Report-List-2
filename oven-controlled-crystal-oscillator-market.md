<p><p>The Oven-Controlled Crystal Oscillator (OCXO) market consists of various types such as Output PECL, Output CMOS, and Output SINEWAVE. Output PECL refers to OCXOs that produce a PECL (Positive Emitter Coupled Logic) signal, which is a high-speed, low-power differential signal primarily used in telecommunications applications. Output CMOS OCXOs generate a CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) signal, a widely used digital logic signal compatible with a variety of electronic devices. Output SINEWAVE OCXOs generate a sine wave signal, which is a fundamental waveform suitable for applications in scientific research, signal processing, and communications.</p></p>
