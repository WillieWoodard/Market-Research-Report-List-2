<p><p>The solid polycarbonate panels market comprises two main types: clear polycarbonate panels and colored/tinted polycarbonate panels. Clear polycarbonate panels are transparent and allow maximum light transmission while offering excellent impact resistance. They are commonly used in applications where natural light is desired, such as greenhouses and skylights. On the other hand, colored/tinted polycarbonate panels are available in various shades and provide both light transmission and privacy. They are often used for decorative purposes and in areas where light control is important, like windows and partitions.</p></p>
