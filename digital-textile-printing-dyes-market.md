Acid inks are used for printing onto silk and wool fabrics due to their ability to create vibrant colors. Disperse inks are ideal for polyester fabrics as they effectively penetrate the fibers and produce excellent colorfastness. Dye-based inks are versatile and can be used on various fabric types, while reactive inks are specifically designed for natural fibers like cotton and linen. Sublimation inks are used for transferring images onto synthetic fabrics through a heat process.</p></p>
