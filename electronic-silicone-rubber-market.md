<p><p>The electronic silicone rubber market consists of two types: transparent and opaque. Transparent silicone rubber refers to a type of material that allows light to pass through, providing clarity and visibility. It is commonly used in applications where transparency is desired, such as LED displays and optical devices. On the other hand, opaque silicone rubber does not permit the transmission of light and is used for applications that require blocking or obscuring of light, such as in electronic casings or power connectors. Both types cater to different needs in the electronic industry.</p></p>
