Carbon-Fiber-Reinforced Polymers (CFRP) offer superior strength-to-weight ratio, making them ideal for structural components. Additionally, other composite materials, such as natural fiber composites and hybrid composites, are gaining attention for their renewable and sustainable characteristics. These composite materials enable the development of efficient and lightweight renewable energy systems.</p></p>
