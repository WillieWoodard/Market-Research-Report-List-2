<p><p>AMOLED Display is a type of organic light-emitting diode that uses an active-matrix technology to produce high-quality images. It offers vivid colors, deep blacks, and fast response times, making it popular in smartphones and televisions. PMOLED, on the other hand, is a passive matrix OLED display that uses a simpler and less expensive technology. It is commonly used in smaller devices like watches and MP3 players. While AMOLED excels in image quality and performance, PMOLED is more suitable for applications where cost and simplicity are prioritized.</p></p>
