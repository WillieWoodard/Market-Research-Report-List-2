<p><p>Clutch Release Bearings are used in vehicle transmissions to engage and disengage the clutch. There are two main types: Pushing-Type Clutch Release Bearings and Pull-Type Clutch Release Bearings. Pushing-Type bearings are designed to push against the clutch cover to release the clutch, while Pull-Type bearings are designed to pull the clutch cover to release the clutch. These different types cater to varying vehicle designs and clutch systems, offering manufacturers flexibility in choosing the most suitable bearing for their transmissions.</p></p>
