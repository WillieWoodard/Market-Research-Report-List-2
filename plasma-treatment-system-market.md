<p><p>The plasma treatment system market consists of two main types: high temperature and low temperature systems. High temperature plasma treatment systems involve the use of extremely high temperatures to generate plasma, typically above 10,000 degrees Celsius. These systems are effective for applications such as surface cleaning, etching, and activation. On the other hand, low temperature plasma treatment systems operate at lower temperatures, generally ranging from 100 to 1000 degrees Celsius. These systems are commonly used for delicate materials and sensitive components, providing a gentler treatment option.</p></p>
