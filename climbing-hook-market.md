<p><p>Climbing hooks come in various market types, including Double Lock, Three-Layer Lock, and Screw Lock. Double Lock hooks feature a mechanism where two separate actions are needed to unlock the gate, ensuring a higher level of security. Three-Layer Lock hooks offer an additional layer of protection with a complex locking system that requires three movements to open the gate. Screw Lock hooks, on the other hand, employ a screwing action that secures the gate tightly, reducing the likelihood of accidental openings. Each type provides climbers with different levels of safety and functionality.</p></p>
