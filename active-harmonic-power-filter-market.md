<p><p>An active harmonic filter is an advanced electronic device used to mitigate harmonic distortions and power factor correction issues in electrical systems. It helps in maintaining the quality of electrical power by eliminating harmonic currents generated by nonlinear loads. Active harmonic filters are typically used in industries with a high level of nonlinear loads such as variable frequency drives, rectifiers, and other power electronic devices.</p><p>The active harmonic filter market is experiencing significant growth due to the increasing need for efficient and reliable power quality solutions. The escalating demand for energy-efficient products and the rising adoption of renewable energy sources are also fueling the market growth. Additionally, stringent government regulations and standards related to power quality and harmonics are driving the market.</p><p>Moreover, the rising usage of power electronic devices and automation technologies in various industries is further propelling the demand for active harmonic filters.
