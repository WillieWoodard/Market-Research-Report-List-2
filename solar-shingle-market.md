<p><p>The solar shingle market can be categorized into two major types: Silicon Photovoltaic (PV) and CIGS Thin Film Photovoltaic. Silicon PV shingles are made from crystalline silicon, which is the most commonly used material for solar cells due to its efficiency and reliability. On the other hand, CIGS thin film PV shingles are made from copper indium gallium selenide, a flexible material that offers higher potential for integration into building design. Both types of solar shingles provide renewable energy solutions by converting sunlight into electricity, contributing to a sustainable and greener future.</p></p>
