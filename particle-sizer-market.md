<p><strong>The Particle Sizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrostatic Classifiers</li><li>Scanning Mobility Particle Sizer</li><li>Aerodynamic Particle Sizer (APS)</li><li>Engine Exhaust Particle Sizer</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Particle sizer market types refer to different instruments used for measuring and analyzing the size distribution of particles in various industries. Electrostatic classifiers use an electric field to separate particles based on their electrical mobility. Scanning Mobility Particle Sizer (SMPS) measures the size distribution of particles in the range of 2-1000 nanometers. Aerodynamic Particle Sizer (APS) determines particle sizes based on their aerodynamic properties. Engine Exhaust Particle Sizer (EEPS) is specifically designed for analyzing particle emissions from engines. Other market types include various instruments catering to specific particle size measurement requirements.</p></p>
