<p><p>The Zinc Oxide Non-linear Resistors (ZNR) market comprises two types: SMD Type and Leaded Type. SMD Type ZNRs are surface-mount devices that are soldered onto the surface of a printed circuit board. They are compact, lightweight, and suitable for high-density electronic applications. On the other hand, Leaded Type ZNRs have leads attached to them, making them easy to connect onto a circuit board. They are preferred for applications where space is not a constraint and where manual soldering is preferred. Both types of ZNRs provide non-linear resistance characteristics and are widely used for overvoltage protection in electronic circuits.</p></p>
