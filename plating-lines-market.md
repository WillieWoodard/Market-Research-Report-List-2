<p><p>The plating lines market comprises various types based on the plating process. Rack plating lines involve suspending products on a rack system for electroplating, suitable for large and irregularly-shaped items. Barrel plating lines involve placing small parts into a barrel for plating, offering high volume production and efficient coverage. Other types can include reel-to-reel plating lines for continuous strip plating, vibratory plating lines for delicate parts, and cathode plating lines with the use of cathodes for specialized applications. Each type caters to different plating needs, providing versatility and flexibility in the plating industry.</p></p>
