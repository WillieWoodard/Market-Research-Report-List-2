The first type is Vehicle Automatic Understanding System, which uses sensors and cameras to identify and authenticate vehicles passing through toll stations. Short Range Communication is another type, where toll collectors communicate directly with onboard units installed in vehicles. Global Position Finding Satellite System utilizes GPS technology to track and calculate vehicle's position for toll collection purposes. Other market types include advanced billing and payment solutions, traffic management systems, and integrated transportation solutions.</p></p>
