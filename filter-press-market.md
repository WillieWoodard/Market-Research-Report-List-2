<p><p>A Filter Press is a specific type of equipment used for solid-liquid separation. It is widely used in industries such as mining, wastewater treatment, chemical, pharmaceutical, and food processing. The filter press operates by applying pressure to the mixture of solids and liquids, forcing the liquid out through a filter medium, while retaining the solids inside the press. This process results in the separation of the liquid and solid components.</p><p>The market for filter presses has been experiencing significant growth in recent years. Plate and Frame Filter Press utilizes separate frames and plates to hold the filter cloth and filter solids. Automatic Filter Press incorporates automation for improved efficiency and reduced manpower.
