<p><strong>The Grinding Mills Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Grinding Mills</li><li>Dry Grinding Mills</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Grinding mills are industrial machines used for breaking solid materials into smaller pieces. Wet grinding mills utilize liquid and operate through the principle of attrition, where the material is ground by rubbing or friction. These mills are useful for processing high moisture and sticky materials. On the other hand, dry grinding mills operate without any liquids involved and rely on the impact and shear forces to break the materials. They are more suitable for grinding dry and hard materials such as ores and coal.</p></p>
