<p><ul><li>Pusher Sorting System</li><li>Carbel Sorting</li><li>Line Shaft Diverter</li><li>Swing Arm Diverter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automated pick dispensers are machines used in various industries to efficiently sort and dispense products. The pusher sorting system utilizes pusher arms to slide items onto different channels, facilitating easy sorting. Carbel sorting systems use a carousel-like mechanism to rotate and select items for dispensing. Line shaft diverters use a central shaft and belts to divert items to desired chutes. Swing arm diverters employ swinging arms to accurately distribute products to different locations. These technologies streamline the picking process, improving productivity and reducing errors.</p></p>
