<p><strong>The Locking Washers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Curved Disc Washer</li><li>Wave Washer</li><li>Split Washer</li><li>Toothed Lock Washer</li><li>Tab Washer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>There are various types of locking washers available in the market. Curved disc washers have a spherical shape that allows them to securely grip the bolt, preventing it from loosening. Wave washers have a wavy shape that creates a spring-like action, producing tension and maintaining the bolt's tightness. Split washers have a split design and exert pressure to prevent rotation. Toothed lock washers have serrations that grip into the mating surface, offering enhanced stability. Tab washers have tabs that bend to secure the bolt, making them suitable for applications with limited space or accessibility.</p></p>
