<p><ul><li>Single Channel</li><li>Double Channel</li><li>Multi-Channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RF rotary joints are devices used to transmit RF signals between stationary and rotating systems. They come in three types: single channel, double channel, and multi-channel. </p><p>A single channel rotary joint has one pathway to transmit signals, making it suitable for applications requiring only one RF signal to be passed. </p><p>A double channel rotary joint has two independent pathways, enabling the simultaneous transmission of two RF signals. </p><p>A multi-channel rotary joint has multiple independent pathways, allowing for the transmission of multiple RF signals simultaneously or at different times. Each type suits specific application needs based on the number of RF signals to be transmitted.</p></p>
