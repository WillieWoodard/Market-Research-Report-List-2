<p><p>Military Tires market types refer to two main categories: OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) and aftermarket. OEM market involves the production and supply of tires directly from the manufacturer to the military vehicle producers. These tires are specifically designed and manufactured to meet the requirements of military vehicles. On the other hand, the aftermarket market involves the sale of tires by third-party vendors or retailers after the vehicle's original purchase. These aftermarket tires may offer different features, sizes, or brands and cater to the specific needs of military customers once the vehicle is already in use.</p></p>
