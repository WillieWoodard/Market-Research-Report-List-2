<p><p>The epoxy resin glass fiber prepreg market can be divided into three types based on the curing temperature: low temperature curing, medium temperature curing, and high temperature curing. Low temperature curing refers to the process where the prepreg is cured at relatively low temperatures, typically below 120 degrees Celsius. Medium temperature curing involves curing at temperatures between 120 to 180 degrees Celsius. High temperature curing, on the other hand, involves curing at temperatures above 180 degrees Celsius. These temperature variations allow for different applications and properties of the epoxy resin glass fiber prepregs.</p></p>
