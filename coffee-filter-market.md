<p><p>Coffee filter market types include permanent filters and paper filters. Permanent filters are typically made of metal or nylon and are reusable. They are popular among environmentally conscious consumers as they reduce waste generated by single-use paper filters. On the other hand, paper filters are disposable and made from porous materials like bleached or unbleached paper. They are widely used due to their convenience, allowing for easy cleanup and disposal after use.
