<p><p>Inline printing press refers to a type of printing machine that operates by printing directly onto a substrate, such as paper or plastic, as it moves through the press. The market for inline printing presses can be classified into two types: manual and automatic. </p><p>A manual inline printing press requires human intervention for tasks like substrate feeding, ink application, and print control. It is appropriate for small-scale operations with limited production volumes. </p><p>On the other hand, an automatic inline printing press performs all tasks automatically without the need for human intervention. It is suitable for high-volume printing operations and offers increased efficiency and productivity.</p></p>
