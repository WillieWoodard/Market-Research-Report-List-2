<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The carbon steel commercial kitchen knife market consists of two types of products: stamped blade products and forged blade products. Stamped blade products are made by cutting the shape of the blade from a sheet of carbon steel and then attaching it to the handle. These knives are usually lighter and more affordable. On the other hand, forged blade products are made by heating the carbon steel and shaping it through a process of continuous hammering. These knives tend to be heavier, more durable, and are often seen as higher-quality options.</p></p>
