In agriculture, it is used to enrich soil with magnesium, promoting plant growth and yield. In healthcare, it is utilized in bath salts, as a laxative for constipation relief, and as a remedy for muscle aches and pains. Moreover, Epsom salt is employed in personal care products like skincare formulations and haircare products due to its exfoliating and cleansing properties.
