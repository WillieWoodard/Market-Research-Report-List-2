<p><p>Lacquer is a type of coating used to provide a shiny and protective finish on various surfaces. There are two main types of lacquer markets: solvent-based lacquer and water-based lacquer. Solvent-based lacquer is made with organic solvents like toluene or xylene and offers excellent durability, making it suitable for surfaces exposed to harsh conditions. Water-based lacquer, on the other hand, is made with water as a solvent and is environmentally friendly. It has lower levels of volatile organic compounds and is ideal for indoor applications or surfaces that require less durability.</p></p>
