<p><p>The isomolded graphite market offers two main types of manufacturing methods: CIP method and Vibration Molding method. The CIP method, also known as Cold Isostatic Pressing, involves applying pressure on a graphite powder-filled mold from all directions to create a solid shape. On the other hand, the Vibration Molding method utilizes vibration and compaction techniques to achieve the desired graphite shape. Both methods provide efficient means of producing isomolded graphite products, catering to the diverse needs of various industries.</p></p>
