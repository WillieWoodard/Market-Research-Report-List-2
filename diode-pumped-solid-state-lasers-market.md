<p><p>Diode pumped solid-state lasers (DPSSL) are classified into two main market types: pulse type and continuous type. Pulse type DPSSL are designed to emit laser beams in short bursts or pulses, with high peak power and short duration. These lasers are suitable for applications requiring high energy output in a short timeframe, such as laser ablation or ultrafast spectroscopy. On the other hand, continuous type DPSSL provide a continuous wave output, making them suitable for applications that require a steady, uninterrupted laser beam, such as laser cutting or medical procedures.</p></p>
